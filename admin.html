📢 Ad Setup Guide

How to Add Ads (Earn Money)

  1. Sign up at Google AdSense (adsense.google.com)
  2. Get approved and copy your Publisher ID (ca-pub-XXXXXXXX)
  3. Paste the AdSense script in <head> of index.html
  4. Replace ad placeholders in the code with your ad units

Alternative Ad Networks (Easier Approval)

  • Adsterra — adsterra.com (easy approval)
  • PropellerAds — propellerads.com
  • Media.net — media.net

Recommended Ad Placements

  • Top banner (728×90) — homepage
  • Before deploy button (300×250) — dashboard
  • Sidebar (160×600) — dashboard
  • Pre-roll video ad before deploy
💡 Users watch an ad before every deploy — this is your main revenue source!
